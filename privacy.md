# Shehryal First Privacy Policy

**Effective date: July 13, 2026**

This policy explains how the Shehryal First desktop application handles information when a creator connects a TikTok account and uses the app to post video content.

## Information we process

With your authorization, the app may receive your TikTok open identifier, basic profile information, granted permission scopes, access tokens, and posting results. It also processes the videos and post details you deliberately select.

## How information is used

Information is used only to authenticate your account, display the connected account, upload or publish content you request, report posting status, maintain security, and troubleshoot errors.

## Sharing

Selected content and required account data are sent to TikTok through TikTok's official APIs to perform your requested action. We do not sell personal information or use it for advertising.

## Storage and security

The app is designed to minimize retained data. Authentication tokens should be stored securely on the creator's device and removed when the account is disconnected. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

## Your choices

You choose which files to post and may cancel before publishing. You may disconnect the account in the app and revoke the app's permissions from TikTok account settings.

## Children

The service is not directed to children and must be used only by people permitted to use TikTok and authorize connected applications.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are published.
